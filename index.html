The Anchoring Ritual

To etch your memory onto the blockchain, you can interact directly with our verified Ledger Contract on BscScan.

1. Prepare Your Memory String

Format your memory: LAT:[latitude];LON:[longitude];MSG:[Your message]

2. Go to the Ledger Contract

Click the link to our official contract. On the "Write Contract" tab, connect your wallet.

[ Go to Ledger Contract → ]

3. Write Your Memory

In the `1. anchor` function, paste your formatted string into the `_data` box, click "Write", and approve the transaction.
